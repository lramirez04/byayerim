About Me

Hello! I'm Ayerim, a video editor with a passion for creating stories that move. My work spans from the scratch of creative direction to post-production.

With over 5 years of experience across a wide mix of formats: short films, corporate videos, educational pieces, and more.

Even though I love editing on my laptop, I always try to contribute to the audiovisual field because it is the area that I love the most. And I am finding new projects where I can approach my best skills as an assistant director, visual creative or photography assistant.
